Sweet Treats

I’m a firm believer that sweets are the best love language.

Why I picked these recipes

I wanted this page to focus on sweet recipes and treats that feel fun, homemade, and realistic to make. These are the kinds of recipes I love because they can be used for holidays, gifts, brunches, or just a night in by myself.

Recent Favorites

Homemade Baileys

I made homemade Baileys as a Christmas gift for loved ones, and it ended up being one of my favorite things I’ve made. It felt thoughtful, festive, and more personal than something store-bought.

Blueberry Muffins

Blueberry muffins are one of my favorite sweet breakfasts. My boyfriend & I made fresh ones for brunch last weekend and I was reminded how much I love them. They are simple & classic.

Cookies

Cookies are one of those desserts that always feel comforting and easy to share. I also think that peanut butter cookies are seriously underrated. Gooey, sweet & best warm!

Sweet recipes I actually want to make again: simple, shareable, and a little special.
