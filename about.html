ABOUT ME

Why I started this site

Before college, I honestly could not make much beyond eggs or a grilled cheese. Once life started getting busier, I realized how helpful it is to know how to cook meals that are simple, affordable, and actually enjoyable. Over time, cooking started to feel like more than just something practical. It became a way to be creative, try new things, and make everyday routines feel a little more fun.

This website is my way of collecting those ideas in one place. I want this site to be helpful for other college students who are still learning to cook and do not want recipes to feel overwhelming. I also love the lifestyle side of food, like baking on weekends, making drinks with friends, and putting together meals that feel a little special without being too complicated.
