DINNERS

Simple meals I’ve actually made

These are a few dinners I’ve made recently that are easy, flavorful, and realistic for busy weeks. Nothing too complicated, just meals that I would actually make again.

GROUND BEEF

Viral Hot Honey Ground Beef Bowls

I finally had to try this meal that's been viral on Tiktok. It's super quick and honestly lives up to the hype. It’s a mix of sweet, spicy, and savory, and it comes together really fast. I like it because it uses simple ingredients but still feels different from my usual dinners. It’s also really easy to customize depending on what I already have at home.

Why I like it:

  • Very fast to make
  • Uses basic ingredients
  • Good for leftovers

CHICKEN SAUSAGE

Creamy Chicken Sausage Orzo

This is one of my favorite one-pot meals because it’s easy and super comforting. Everything cooks together, which makes cleanup really simple. It’s creamy without being too heavy, and the chicken sausage adds a lot of flavor without needing much extra seasoning.

What makes it easy:

  • Only one pot needed
  • Minimal prep work
  • Great for weeknights

GROUND TURKEY

Ground Turkey Stir Fry

This is one of my go-to meals when I want something quick but still balanced. It’s really easy to throw together with vegetables and a simple sauce. I like that it feels healthy but still flavorful, and it’s a good option when I want something lighter.

Why I make this often:

  • Quick and flexible ingredients
  • Good balance of protein and veggies
  • Easy to meal prep
