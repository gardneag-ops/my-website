Build Your Smoothie

Smoothie Tips

Use frozen fruit, less liquid, Greek yogurt, or banana.

Orange juice makes it sweeter, almond milk makes it creamier, and coconut water makes it lighter.

Add protein yogurt, peanut butter, oats, or chia seeds.
